ACME Corporation - Employee Handbook 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to ACME Corporation, the world's leading supplier of cartoon anvils and rocket-powered roller skates. This handbook covers the essential policies for all employees working at the Grand Canyon facility.

2. Work Hours and Attendance
- Core hours are from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Thursday.
- Employees are expected to log a minimum of 32 hours per week.
- "Rocket-skate Fridays" are mandatory for all field testing staff.

3. Health and Safety
- Hard hats must be worn at all times in the Anvil Manufacturing Wing.
- Do not stand underneath suspended pianos, safes, or giant boulders.
- If you encounter a roadrunner on company property, do not attempt to engage it. Contact the local Animal Control immediately.
- Giant rubber bands are strictly for testing purposes and should not be used in the cafeteria.

4. Benefits
- ACME offers a comprehensive health plan covering explosive injuries and flattening by heavy objects.
- Employees receive 20 days of paid time off per year.
- The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 5%.
- Free birdseed is provided in the breakroom on Wednesdays.

5. Expense Reporting
All expenses related to purchasing giant magnets, earthquake pills, or tornado kits must be approved by W. E. Coyote, Chief Testing Officer, prior to purchase. Submissions without prior approval will not be reimbursed.
